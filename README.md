# baycation-cse470
Project Title: Baycation


Brief Description: Baycation is a social travel platform that connects
passionate travelers with like-minded companions, trusted tour guides, and
unique travel experiences. Whether you're a solo adventurer seeking a
travel buddy, a group looking to plan the perfect trip, or a tour guide hosting
unforgettable journeys—Baycation brings your travel tribe together.
From discovering group trips and renting gear to sharing stories, reviews,
and connecting through interest-based travel communities—Baycation
makes planning and experiencing travel social, seamless, and fun.
You don’t just travel with Baycation—you belong.

Requirement Analysis:

1. Users can create and manage personal accounts with travel preferences.

2. Travelers can find and connect with potential travel buddies.

3. Users can create, edit, and share trip plans and itineraries.
4. Group members can collaboratively plan and discuss trips in real-time.
5. Users can post stories, photos from their travels.
6. Travelers can like, comment on, and follow other users' content.
7. Users can rate and review guides, trips, and destinations.
8. Travelers can browse and purchase or rent travel gear through a marketplace.
9. Tour guides can create and manage hosted trip listings with full details.
10. Guides can interact with travelers through chat and Q&A.
11. Guides can accept or reject trip bookings from users.
12. Guides can receive public ratings and build credibility.
13. Guides can list gear for rent or sale on the platform.
14. Admins can manage users, content, and reports from a central dashboard.
15. Admins can approve or reject guide verification requests.
16. Admins can monitor system analytics and performance.
